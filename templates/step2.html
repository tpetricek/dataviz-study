Background information

Before you start, please maximize your browser window and switch off any devices and applications that might be distracting such as your phone, e-mail client or music. Please make sure that you are completing the study in a space that allows you to focus for 15 minutes required to complete the study.

Next, enter your Prolific ID. You can find this at the top of this webpage or by going to your account page. Additionally, please provide the basic background information required below.


When you are ready, please click the "Next" button. You will be shown the first article, followed by the second article, followed by a questionnaire. After completing the questionnaire, you will be shown a brief summary of the study and a link to complete the Prolific survey.
