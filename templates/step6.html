Study description

The purpose of the study was to explore how data visualizations and interactive data visualizations help readers understand and remember news stories and whether they make news stories more engaging. The study had three different versions of the first article. One with an interactive visualization, one with static visualization and one with just an illustrative image. The second article was the same for everyone. The purpose of our research is to assess which of the three methods provides the most effective way of communicating information.
