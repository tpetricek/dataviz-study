Thank you for joining our study

Please read the following important information before starting the study. We ask you to complete this survey, which will take approximately 15 minutes in one go. In other words, please take the survey now only if you have 15 minutes that you can dedicate to it. Thank you very much.

Study instructions

In this study, you will be asked to read two newspaper articles. After that, you will be asked to answer a couple of questions about the two articles. The news stories have been selected among recent newsworthy events and should not cause any emotional harm or distress to the reader.

Consent form

I consent to participate in this session, which will involve reading two articles and answering six questions. I understand that all data will be kept confidential by the researcher. No personally identifiable information will be collected during the study and my responses cannot be linked to my personal identity. I am free to withdraw at any time without giving a reason. I consent to the publication of study results as long as the information is anonymous so that no participants could be identified.

The study has received approval from the Ethics Advisory Group of The Alan Turing Institute and by the University of Kent.
